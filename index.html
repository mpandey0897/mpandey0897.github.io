About Me

Hello there, and a warm welcome to my virtual abode! As a postdoctoral researcher in the Stochastics group at Aarhus University, I work on problems at the interface of probability theory, random graph models, and stochastic geometry. My current research explores the behavior of spatially embedded networks governed by Gibbsian interactions, with a focus on understanding their structural and probabilistic properties.
