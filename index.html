About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Stochastics Group at Aarhus University. My work lies at the interface of probability theory, random graph models, and stochastic processes. I focus on spatially embedded networks with Gibbsian interactions, emphasizing large deviation principles, rare events, and limiting behavior.

I also teach graduate-level courses in probability theory and random complex networks. I completed my PhD at Eindhoven University of Technology (TU/e) within the NETWORKS program, affiliated with the Probability group.
