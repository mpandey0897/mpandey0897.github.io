More About Me

I hail from Nainital (pictured above), a stunning lake town nestled amidst the mountains in northern India. The name comes from its distinguishing feature — an eye-shaped lake — with “Naini” meaning eye and “Tal” meaning lake.

I was born in Ranikhet, and later my parents relocated to Nainital, where I spent my childhood alongside my sister. I have a fondness for playing almost every sport, with badminton holding a special place as one of my favorites.

Quick facts

  • From: Nainital (born in Ranikhet)
  • Mountains & lakes person
  • Sports fan — especially badminton
